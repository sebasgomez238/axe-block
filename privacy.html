Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

üîí Your Privacy is Our Priority

Axe Block is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or data from our users.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Axe Block is a simple, fun game that respects your privacy. We want to be completely transparent about what we do and don't collect:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identification information.
  • Device Information: We do not access or collect device identifiers, operating system information, or hardware details.
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect your location information in any way.
  • Usage Analytics: We do not track how you play the game, your scores, progress, or gameplay patterns.
  • Advertising Data: We do not collect data for advertising purposes or share information with advertisers.
  • Social Media Information: We do not access or integrate with social media accounts.
  • Camera or Microphone: We do not access your device's camera or microphone.
  • Contacts or Photos: We do not access your contacts list or photo library.

Data Storage

Since we don't collect any data, we don't store any data about you on our servers or anywhere else. Any game progress or settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

Axe Block does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or social media platforms. The game operates completely independently without external connections.

Children's Privacy

Our game is safe for children of all ages. Since we don't collect any information whatsoever, there are no privacy concerns related to children's data under COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) or any other privacy regulations.

Internet Connection

Axe Block does not require an internet connection to play. The game functions completely offline, ensuring that no data is transmitted even if your device is connected to the internet.

Updates to This Policy

If we ever change our approach to privacy (which we don't anticipate), we will update this policy and notify users through the App Store update notes. However, our commitment is to maintain this no-data-collection approach.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data about you:

  • There is no data to request or download
  • There is no data to delete
  • There is no data to correct or modify
  • There is no data being processed or shared

Axe Block is designed to provide a pure, distraction-free gaming experience without any privacy concerns. You can enjoy the game knowing that your privacy is completely protected.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • GitHub Issues: Create an issue on our GitHub repository
  • Repository: Visit our GitHub page

Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Apple App Store Guidelines
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations

Since we collect no data, compliance with these regulations is straightforward - there's simply no personal data to protect, process, or manage.
